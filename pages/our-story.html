About Us

Our Story

A small planet born from exhaustion, love, and the quiet determination to find yourself again.

From One Mother's Journey

When our son arrived two years ago, the world as I knew it was lovingly dismantled. Every hour, every thought, every impulse became his. And for a while, that felt right — beautiful, even. But somewhere in the sleepless months that followed, I noticed a quiet fading. Not of love, but of me.

I had forgotten what it felt like to do something just because it brought me joy. To steep a cup of tea slowly and actually taste it. To fold paper, light incense, write a sentence just for myself.

Me-Time Planet started as a WeChat account — a small corner of the internet where I could document these small acts of reclaiming. A cup of matcha here. A crafting afternoon there. A note on why traditional Chinese art forms feel like a balm for the modern mind.

It grew quietly, the way the best things do. Readers reached out to say "me too." And so a platform became a community, and a community became a purpose.

What We Believe

We believe rest is not a luxury. We believe that creating something with your hands — whether it is a fish lantern or a bowl of floating lacquer — is one of the most grounding things a person can do. We believe a good cup of tea, prepared with intention, is a genuine act of self-care.

We believe mothers, caregivers, and busy women deserve a planet of their own.

Welcome to ours.
